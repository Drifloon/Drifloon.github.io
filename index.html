Trip to Japan - chapter 2

Happy to see my fan(s?) here! With the time goes, I have written some about some interesting things during the trip. However, due to my eccentricity of pursuing some extreme perfect on aesthtics. I decided to revise them and try it arrange them in a more eye-catching way. So sorry for my readers, もしわけござなる！

30th Sept

I had no idea about what we had done yesterday night, especially what we played, on condition that we were all drunk seriously. In the morning, nono got up very early in that she gonna go to the company. And I saw her very offcial dressing, kind of kakkoii(Sorry for no pictures, I should have taken it!) What about me? hmm.. sleeping in the bed till about 10am.

After getting up, seeing the foods nono make for me, I am totally moved…..How nice she is!（I cannot praise her more, so that is enough) After finishing the meal, I was very reluctant to wash them, more willing to leave them alone and sleep again, but I do not want to spend rest of the day on the roadway…

Nono was back at noon, while I was writing homework for killing time(I hate studying). She has classes in the afternoon, so it would better for us to eating lunch in her university, the University of Tokyo, at Komaba Campus.

The dining hall was awesome, why? Because its awesomeness. I really liked the atmosphere in the hall where is bright and clean, filled with students sitting in a group and chatting. That is the life in university!

Campus in Komaba is for low grade(Freshmen) students who are excited and curious about the college life. When I was walking around, I ca see some practising dancing(in a sudden), hearing the voice of saxophone, doing exerising in the Gym, just totally involved what they keen on. I can feel school’s respect of all the students hobbies and students’ ardor of trying new things.

The real intention to write a lot above, was to dilute my failure on shooting games, I must admitted her shooting skills is outstanding to some extent.

After class, we met at Meidaimae and then we headed to Shinjuku, which was my first choice of the city in Japan. Why? I have many unforgetable memories here and it has many departments and restaurants, very prosperious state combining with some Adult only store, I did not know why children cannot get in though, that was unfair!

We went to one of the biggest bookstore here, it is called kinokuniya? And for satisfied her wish of eating kaoya, we went to one luxurious chinese restaurants called Tokyodaihenden here. To tell the truth, I think XX NO.1 can beat on the taste of every dishes I ordered and I promise to her I gonna let her eat kaoya in a version of betterment when she comes to China. Japanese awareness of seperation ubiquitously exist, even on the dining table.

So far so good.

Trip to Japan - Chapter 1

27th Sept

Nono the ‘Kaicho’ invited me to Japan when I was in Berkeley with her, so I planned and prepared the whole trip for a long time. Finally I am going to take off to Japan, though I suffered a lot from preparing material for visa.(due to my dilatory merit.x.)

It is the third time I have come to Japan. The first time was in my elementary school, when I was a little kid, I went to Hiroshima and Fukuoka, as a member of a kinder culture exchange activity (It was about 书法, at that time I was VERY good at it). I also took part in the memorial activity of atomic bomb explosion in Hiroshima.

And the second time, when I was in sophomore, I took part in a short time travelling project which was organized by a company and Waseda University. Together with other people, I went to Tokyo, then Yokohama, Hakone, Kyoto and Osaka. I spent very very unforgetable time and met many kind friends during the trip.

Let’s get to the point, This time I made a very bold choice: Take plane in the midnight. The most crucial reason is that I want to try ANA’s airplane, because I heard the company has top-class service and also delicious food :P. But it came out to be very expensive (partly because too many chinese people will go to Japan during the holiday). So as a poor student, I had to choose a less expensive flight that was to fly at midnight.

The airplane…


Episode: There was a fraudulent message send to me when I just arrived the airport. It said I did not pay for the ticket and my ticket had been cancelled!



eating at that time though…

I was very surprised cuz I did not know what should I say to nono if I cannot go to Japan just before the day I should leave. So I asked for help to my mom, and she was surprised too. However, she contacted with officials
and verified my ticket had been paid, and then we realized it was sent by frauds. I was relieved and just want to say..

29th Sept

Early get up! Today I get up very early cuz we have many places to go. Finally $tourist$’s day comes!

After changing several metros, we get to the first place ‘Tsukiji‘. Tsukiji is famous for its market, there maybe one of the biggest transaction centers of seafood in the world. I knew here in that I watched some japanese food documentaries, which mentioned Tsukiji.





Makkah for (sea)food lover’s

very delicious but expensive…

While Tsukiji is near Ginza, kind of shopping district, where roads are arranged like Manhattan in New York. So We head to Ginza as the next station. And we just walk around.
