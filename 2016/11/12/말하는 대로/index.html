말하는 대로

Today I just recalled a Korean song, which is my very favorite one, called ‘말하는 대로’ in Korean and ‘言之命至’ in Chinese. I don’t remember why I can recall it but when I just thought the lyric of the song, my tear almost went out… So today I repeat it for many times, and I think it is good for showing you the story of how I met with the song and the context of it.

I am a big fan of a Korean TV show ‘무한도전’(‘Infinite Challenge’ in English, ‘无限挑战’ in Chinese), though someone cannot believe my love of it(maybe in her eyes, the show will attract only girls and potential gay..). The song is from it, sung by one of the main character called ‘유재석’(‘Jae Seok Yoo’ in English, ‘刘在石’ in Chinese) who I admire very much. There is a tradition of Infinite Challenge that the program will hold a music festival every two years. In the very beginning, it was held under an overpass near river and the number of the audience was less than one hundred. But during its increasing development, it at most attracted eighty thousands people in total. This songs is from music festival in 2011.

Surprisingly, The song was not included in the official scene, but when every audience went back, only Jae Seok, and his partner Juk Lee(In that year, they played as a band called “Sagging Snail”), played that songs on the stage. It was just like a song which sings to Yoo himself, cuz the content of it was all based on Yoo’s 20th living experience. It describe the struggle and the strong willing to succeed while facing to perplexing future in his youth.
